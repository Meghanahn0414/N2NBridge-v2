Hostinger drops out of production: app + DB must be co-located to avoid cross-cloud latency & egress, and the app tier itself handles PII — so empanelment covers the whole stack, not just the database. Keep Hostinger for dev / pilot only; for compliant production the empanelled cloud becomes your entire environment.

How to choose

Pick Postgres / MariaDB if…
you want relational integrity, cheaper long-term hosting, SQL reporting, and abundant talent — and can absorb a 3–5 week rewrite once.
Pick self-host Mongo if…
compliance / empanelment is the only goal, you don't want a rewrite, and you can staff the ongoing ops (or buy their managed-DB add-on).
